About us

The Curve Consultants is the Project Planning & Management Consultancy organization since 2007 based in NOIDA-DELHI NCR. The Curve aims to become a leading project planning & management consultancy service provider by adopting sustainable and advance technologies in construction industries. Our mission is to create an effective bondage between the clients, flexibility as well as responsibility towards the work we get involved in. Our Approaches are “Satisfaction of client to achieve the desired goal within the scheduled time and budget.
We work towards getting better with the changing trends and technological enhancements in the environment so as to provide the clientele with the latest coverage of the work they ask for. Keeping our organization updated about the changing technologies is one of the crucial aspects we practice. It is an essential part that we consider various types of constraints while working on any project. We surely optimize to get into the top priorities of clients when we are managing the project, we also figure out the constraints, the clients face in further completion of the project. Hence, making our organization a fruitful one.

Our Way Of Services

At our company, we pride ourselves on delivering exceptional service through a blend of innovation, reliability, and customer-centric approaches. Our way of services is characterized by:

Project Closure

✅ Punch list De-snagging.
✅ Compiling of as- built documentation.
✅ Commissioning and Project Handover.

Construction Stage

✅ Site Supervision and Management as per Contract Specifications
✅ Management and coordination with Client on Technical Matters
✅ Health, Safety and Environment (HSE) Management
✅ Monitor Sub-Contractors Site Establishment
✅ Monitor Sub-Contractors Resource Input Levels
✅ Monitoring of progress
✅ Management of Variations
✅ Management of Construction Drawings issued by the Client
✅ Contract Administration and Assistance in Dispute Resolution
✅ Recommend Rates for Unpriced Works
✅ Certification of sub-contractor’s/Vendor’s Bill
✅ Conduct site meeting

Pre-Construction Stage

✅ Establishing A Reporting And Documentation Management System.
✅ Preparation Of Project Master Programme.
✅ Management And Coordination With Clients On Technical Matters.
✅ Preparation Of Quality Assurance Plan.
✅ Review And Assist, If Required, For Finalization Of Contract Packages And Contract Conditions For The Appointment Of Sub-contractors

Planning Stage

✅ Study the physical, demographical, and topographical data as well as the boundary & surroundings of the project.
✅ After studying the data, the project is planned accordingly.
✅ Schedule, Budgeting, available an proposed resources, and constraints are considered in the planning process.

Our working process

Why “Us” must be hired?

Analysing feasibility of any project proposed

No Pmc (only Client/ Owner Involvement)

Only clients/owner with the team of their colleagues are involved . in this phase the feasibility of the project is decided and discussed with them only . Whatever the decision taken is moreover focussed on the basis of boundaries and limitation set by the owner with the people of same boundaries.

The Curve Team – Pmc Hired (client/owner And Team Of Experts Involvement)

The client with the team of experts discusses about the project and other perspectives i.e Marketability of the project & know your customers, pros and cons of the project, execution limitation, resources availability and their constraints, Location dynamics,

No Pmc (only Client/Owner Involvement)

Most of the time Only Architects is hired and budget & time required for the project can not be discussed in detail. Drawings are provided in phased manner , many of the things may be left and not taken care of, Coordination of Drawings can be difficult because all the drawings are not provided by the Architect in first instance. Most of the cases Detail inventory/BOQ is not prepared in advance and detail budgeting and uses of material, finishing material and designer material can not be discussed in advance because client /owner may have the lacking of exposure of all type of resources to be used in project.

The Curve Team – Pmc Hired (client/owner And Team Of Experts Involvement)

The Curve- MC has the team of expert Civil Engineers/Planning Engineers/ MEP Engineers. Hence Whatever the drawings are provided may be studied and analysed by the experts and /if there is any doubt or insufficiency -same may be discussed with the Architects and other designing consultants in advance. Drawings are provided in Phase manner : The Budget , required time /schedule of project to be completed may be analysed in detail. Required approvals may be coordinated with the architects and authority with client Required budget and Detail of Schedule of project execution using standards templates (Gantt Chart etc) may be prepared with the client based on the availability of budget and other propositions.

No Pmc (only Client/Owner Involvement)

In most of the Cases :
Contactor is hired by the Client /owner.
Technical Professional/Engineers is hired by the Contractor only.
Client don’t hire the technical professional, they hire/deploy only one person to look after or sharing the site information to client
Quality control is done the contractor only.
Architects visit the project site occasionally and regular monitoring and quality control may not be done by the experts regularly.
Budget /Billing : Detail billing can not be monitored/checked by the experts , Bill prepared by the contractor is only document to release the payment and there may be no re-check/control tool to the bill.
Quality standards may not checked by experts and whatever the quality is being executed by the contractor will be acceptable to the bill.

The Curve Team – Pmc Hired (client/owner And Team Of Experts Involvement)

Team of Experts :
Deploy the qualified engineer /experts during the execution of project
Experts may be available during the entire period of project execution.
Quality may be checked and controlled by the experts at each and every stage of execution of projects.
If there is any change/modification required during the execution- may be discussed with the architects/ designers and same may be incorporated in execution.
Execution of work may be controlled based on the availability of fund/budget with the client.
During the execution all the experts may visit the site and give their opinion /depute the expert during the execution of particular type of work.
Project has the Civil Work, MEP work , landscaping work – in these cases PMC may depute the professional expert in particular field .
The progressive planning for next month/quarter etc may be provided to client in advance .
Marketing/sales : Team of expert may submit the progress /planning required for the marketing /sales team.
All the stakeholders : Marketing & Sales, Customers, Client expectation : Regular meeting or discussion may be held to fulfil the expectation of all the stakeholders and work may be executed accordingly

No Pmc (only Client/Owner Involvement)

Whatever the work is executed
All the work is checked by the client /Architects only .
Ommissioning of all the services may not be checked /monitored completely in detail by the client / Architect

The Curve Team – Pmc Hired (client/owner And Team Of Experts Involvement)

Whatever the work is executed
All the work is checked by the client /Architects as well as by the team of expert engineers and professionals.
Ommissioning of all the services may be checked by the experts in detail.
Experts may ensure to client that the work is completed as planned or if changed the same may also be discussed with the client
